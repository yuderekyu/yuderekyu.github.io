Review

Homeland Elegies

Ayad Akhtar


5/5

A shock to the system. Ayad is sharp, captivating, personable, tearing down idealized American values and exposing their true, disturbing nature. Add to the mix being of Pakistani/Muslim heritage in a post 9/11 United States, and you have a distinctive, potent account of being in America. My pick for 2020 book of the year.
